Favorite Books According to Kayla

Top 5 Books of 2024

Queen of Shadows by Sarah J Maas

The fourth instalment of the Throne of Glass series is a great shift from world-building to action.

The House Beyond the Dunes by Mary Burton

This psychological thriller had an unexpected and wild twist that I still think about to this day.

The Wicked King by Holly Black

The second novel in the Folk of the Air series delivers a shocking plot twist at the end of the book, leaving me eager to read the final book in the series.

Throne of Glass by Sarah J Maas

The first book in the Throne of Glass series was the perfect blend of world-building and action.

The Long Game by Elena Armas

This book had all of my favorite elements: broody sweet guy, soccer, and enemies to lovers.
